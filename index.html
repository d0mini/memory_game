Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

The more pairs you match, the more personalised party stickers will be delivered to your door! fill your room with stickers praising your personal achievements!

Delivery of stickers subject to correct address, bank, passport and all other personal legal and financial documentation.
